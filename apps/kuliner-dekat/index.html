This Privacy Policy explains how Kuliner Dekat ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") handles information when you use the application. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Depending on the features used, the App may collect, process, or store the following information:

  • Device or technical information needed for app performance, crash diagnostics, security, fraud prevention, and advertising.
  • Optional permission-based data, such as camera, microphone, files, storage, location, notifications, or contacts, only when the relevant feature is used and permission is granted.

Many features store data locally on the user device. If the App includes backup, sharing, export, cloud, or third-party integration features, data may be processed according to the user selected action and the relevant third-party service.

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services, including Google AdMob, to display advertisements and help maintain app quality. These services may collect and process data such as device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, app interaction data, diagnostic information, and other technical data for advertising, analytics, security, and fraud prevention.

Third-party services process data according to their own privacy policies and terms. Users can manage advertising identifiers and privacy controls through their device settings where available.

4. How Information Is Used

Information may be used to provide and improve app features, save user preferences and content, enable reminders or reports, display advertisements if ads are included, diagnose errors, prevent abuse, and maintain app performance.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell users personal information. Data may be shared or processed with third-party services used by the App, such as advertising, analytics, crash reporting, cloud storage, or platform services, only as needed to provide and improve the App. We may also disclose information if required by law or legal process.

6. Data Storage and Retention

Locally stored data remains on the user device until the user deletes it, clears app data, or uninstalls the App. Data handled by third-party services is retained according to the policies of those services.

7. Data Deletion

Users may delete locally stored data by using delete features inside the App, clearing app data from device settings, or uninstalling the App. For data handled by third-party services, users should refer to the privacy controls provided by those services.

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 unless specifically stated otherwise in the app listing. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us.

We take reasonable steps to protect information handled by the App. However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
